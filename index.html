About me

Hi! Welcome to My Site!

I have recently completed my B.Sc. degree in Theoretical Physics at the Shahjalal University Of Science And Technology, Bangladesh, graduating in March 2024. My undergraduate project was on investigating the electronic properties of transitional metal oxide using density functional theory in the quantum espresso packages under the supervision of Dr. Enamul Haque (Associate Professor, Department of Physics, SUST).

My current research/project: I am currently working as a research assistant in Computational Material Science at the High-Performance Computing Lab (HPC Lab) of the Department of Physics, SUST. My current project involves utilizing time-dependent density functional theory to evaluate the optical properties of a metal oxide and comparing the result with experimental data that were obtained from the Laser Ablation method. The experiment was conducted at the Non-linear Optics Lab at SUST. I am also conducting another project under the supervision of Dr. Abdullah Al Fahad, (Climate Physicist, NASA GSFC) to predict the track of tropical cyclones six, twelve, and twenty-four hours in advance using a deep learning method.
